Case Study: Bakery Expansion Success
Client: SweetRise Bakery, Atlanta GA

Challenge: SweetRise Bakery was thriving locally, but limited equipment and staffing held them back. They needed at least $25K to purchase new ovens and hire additional team members, but traditional loans felt risky and expensive.

Approach: We conducted in-depth research and identified five small business grants that prioritized women- and minority-owned food businesses. We crafted a compelling proposal that emphasized the bakery’s community impact, local sourcing, and job creation.

Solution: By highlighting their unique story and measurable outcomes, we positioned SweetRise as a perfect fit for competitive grant opportunities. We guided them through revisions, compliance, and submission, ensuring every requirement was met.

Results: Within 60 days, SweetRise secured a $10,000 grant award. They invested in new baking equipment and expanded their team by two employees, increasing production capacity by 35%. This not only boosted revenue but also allowed them to serve larger catering contracts.

“We couldn’t have done this without Whitney’s grant expertise. The process felt overwhelming until she broke it down step by step. Now we’re expanding faster than ever.”

Maria L., Owner of SweetRise Bakery
Case Study: Fitness Studio Growth
Client: Flex & Flow Fitness, Charlotte NC

Challenge: The studio wanted to add holistic wellness classes and purchase recovery equipment but lacked the $20,000 they needed. Traditional financing options felt risky for their small team.

Approach: We researched opportunities for health and wellness businesses focused on community impact. We highlighted Flex & Flow’s mission to improve accessibility for underserved communities and promote overall wellness.

Solution: We tailored their grant proposal to emphasize measurable outcomes such as new classes offered, projected community participation, and job creation through hiring additional instructors.

Results: Flex & Flow Fitness secured a $15,000 grant in under 90 days. With the funds, they launched two new wellness programs, purchased recovery equipment, and grew their membership base by 40% within six months.

“This grant gave us the confidence and resources to dream bigger. Whitney’s guidance turned what felt impossible into reality.”

Jasmine R., Co-Owner of Flex & Flow Fitness
Case Study: Tech Startup Boost
Client: CodeSpark Solutions, Austin TX

Challenge: The startup had a promising prototype but needed at least $30,000 to complete development and hire technical staff. Investors were hesitant without traction, and bootstrapping wasn’t enough.

Approach: We identified technology-focused grant programs that prioritized innovation and diversity in tech. We positioned CodeSpark’s product as a high-impact tool solving real problems in workforce automation.

Solution: We wrote a data-backed proposal that highlighted industry need, potential economic impact, and the founder’s unique perspective as a Black entrepreneur in tech.

Results: CodeSpark Solutions won a $25,000 grant, allowing them to finalize product development and onboard two full-time developers. Within six months, they launched their beta product and secured their first paying clients.

“Whitney helped us see how to position our story and data to stand out. That $25,000 gave us the runway we needed to go from idea to product launch.”

Andre T., Founder of CodeSpark Solutions
